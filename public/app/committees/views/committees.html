Liazon Committees

The Liazon Celebration & Service Committee's mission is focused around three core components:

  • Employee Recognition and Engagement
  • Community Outreach and Volunteerism
  • Team Building

The Celebration & Service Committee is comprised of the following 4 sub-committees:

Fun & Games Committee

Cultural Diversity & Community Outreach Committee

Wellness Committee

Beautification Committee

{{::vm.committee.text}} Committee

The Fun & Games Committee is dedicated to promoting friendliness, familiarity, and increased morale through social experiences and enjoyable activities that are open and accessible to all.

The Cultural Diversity and Community Outreach Committee is dedicated to working on engaging the workforce in cultural diversity awareness. Identifying Cultural community events, working on ethnic heritage months etc.

The Wellness Committee is dedicated to providing opportunities for employees to live a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Through our leadership, collaboration and teamwork we will develop fun and supportive programs that will include options through which they can empower their mind, body and spirit. The Liazon wellness committee will inspire our company to participate in a commitment to health and well-being through various education programs and activities that will enable them to work towards achieving living a healthy lifestyle both in and out of the workplace.

The Beautification Committee is dedicated to enhancing, restoring & maintaining the beauty of our Buffalo and NYC offices. The committee will look to add art work, establish a "Kudos Wall", maintain floral arrangements around the offices and otherwise decorate and improve the look of the offices overall.

Organization Chart

The directory allows you to view snap-shot of the organization chart based on a particular employee.

Employee View

Selecting an employee from the list will display the following:

  • The employee's photo, name & title
  • The employee's manager (photo, name & title)
  • The employee's direct reports (photo, name & title)
You can direct link to a particular employee's organization chart snap-shot by adding the employee ID at the end of the org chart url
  • http://directory.liazon.corp/employees/org-chart/1209764

Clicking on the employee will take you to the profile of that employee.

Clicking on the employee's manager will display the organization chart snap-shot for the manager.

Clicking on an employee's direct report will display the organization chart snap-shot for the direct report.
